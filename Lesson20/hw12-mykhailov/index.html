Monstera

Monstera deliciosa, the Swiss cheese plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical forests of southern Mexico, south to Panama. It has been introduced to many tropical areas, and has become a mildly invasive species in Hawaii, Seychelles. It is very widely grown in temperate zones as a houseplant.

Clover

Clovers occur in most temperate and subtropical regions of the world, except Southeast Asia. The plants are useful as livestock feed and can be planted as a cover crop or used as a green manure. The flowers are highly attractive to bees, and clover honey is a common secondary product of clover cultivation.

Vertical gardening with real plants

Vertical gardening technology saves usable floor space, allows you to give phytowalls different shapes and set different sizes. The vertical gardening system operates in automatic mode. Fitowalls can be one-sided or two-sided, in the form of a column, pyramid, cube; fill niches, bay windows, loggias; used as partitions.

Green moss walls

Phytowall made of natural moss is a unique novelty on the design and decor market. Moss is very pleasant to the touch and absorbs sound perfectly. The wall can be ordered in any shape, in various sizes and colors. The exclusivity of this material is that its technical characteristics make it possible to bring to life almost any design idea.

Phytopaintings and phytomodules

A phytomodule is a type of phytowall that differs from it in some design features. It does not require connection to the water supply and sewerage system, you can move it from place to place without loss. The phytomodule can serve as both an interior decoration and a double-sided partition. Convenient for modular display designs, office and living room decorations, and any other portable product.
